ABOUT ME


Malik Kolade is a Product Designer with over 2 years of experience designing digital products for businesses and startups. He has designed over 10 deployed solutions across education, circular economy, business, arts, fintech, and so on.

He is an Alumnus of Fate Foundation, Utiva Design School, and Design School, SkillNG. He was the Winner of The Bridge Pitch Challenge, 2020, and Co-winner of Facebook & CcHub Design for Epidemiology Challenge, 2021. He is an assistant mentor at Kimoyo Fellowship and a Fellow of SprinNG Mentorship Programme.

When he is not creating responsive user interface, he is writing about life experiences in a way to arouse the consciousness of the society towards a better mental health of its inhabitants.
